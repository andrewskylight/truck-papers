Refusal to issue CVOR certificate

So, you did your homework and aced the CVOR (Commercial Vehicle Operator's Registration) test, only to receive a letter informing you that your CVOR license has been refused. You may not even fully understand why. No worries, I've done with this successfully in the past. There is nothing like seeing a once frustrated and worries business owner happy and smiling.

I have many years of experience working with the Ministry of Transportation and understanding the intricacies of the CVOR system. I'll work with you to understand why your license was refused and develop a strategy for appealing the decision.

I'll guide you through the appeals process, ensuring that all necessary documentation is completed accurately and on time, which includes the safety plan. We'll also provide ongoing support and assistance to ensure that you're able to operate your business with the confidence and peace of mind that comes with being CVOR compliant.

By choosing to work with me, you'll benefit from my extensive knowledge of the CVOR system and the commitment to providing exceptional customer service. We understand the importance of your business's success and will work tirelessly to help you achieve your goals.
